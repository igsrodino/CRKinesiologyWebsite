Couple's Consultation

2 hrs | $240

In a couple’s consultation the approach is first to understand the points of view of each partner towards the issue that made the relationship face a challenge.

It is not uncommon for couples to feel overwhelmed and overloaded trying to balance a healthy relationship in today’s busy life. Usually we forget who we are and why did we get into the relationship from the beginning.

This therapy helps to unlock any communicative blocks and clear all the emotional attachments, meanings and tensions. We work on understanding and clear communication, care and respect, affection and physical intimacy developing positive changes to improve problem solving skills.
